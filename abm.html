ABM Strand

The ABM Strand is for those who plan to take up business-related courses in higher education or engage in business, entrepreneurship, and other business-related careers.
The Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) strand focuses on the basic concepts of financial management, business management, corporate operations, and all things that are accounted for.
